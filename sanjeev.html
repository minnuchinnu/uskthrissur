WS03

Abstraction through Acrylic Medium

-Sanjeev Joshi

- Pune, Maharashtra, India

-Urban Sketching in Acrylic Medium. Or Abstraction through Acrylic Medium

Workshop Description

I have not seen any urban sketcher using acrylic paint for urban sketching. Hence I wish to teach how to use acrylic medium cleverly, quickly and effectively in urban sketching. For this, I use plastic knives and oil papers as tools for on- location sketch painting. The style of sketches will be abstract realism as we would be using all broad tools. I will be supplying plastic knives and wooden tools.

Supply List

-White textured paper (oil paper) A4 size - min 6nos.

-Acrylic paints in small tubes- red, blue and yellow

-Bowl for water.
