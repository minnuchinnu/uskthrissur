Sketching Stories of People and Places

-Manish Pimple

-USK Pune

-Sketching Stories of People and Places

Workshop Description

We're all part of the stories around us- connected to people, places, and moments. But how do we draw these experiences? This workshop helps choose what and how to draw using basic tools. It's about discovering what makes the place special and capturing stories. We'll learn to highlight the unique attraction of specific place or thing in our sketches, frame the story with people and places, closing on the drama in scene. Human activity defines a place just as much as the building or the architecture. We cannot imagine a city without its people. Sketcher may chose to ignore all human noise or filter out people to make great timeless sketch, but the essence of a space and experience is incomplete without people. Choose from the architecture, scale, elements such as storefronts, signage, canopies, patterns, facades etc. and from the people the pulse of life, activity that will make our sketch lively. Explore how injecting a sense of playfulness can spice up the sketch while capturing these defining characteristics. The workshop is to introduce the process in discovering the narratives and energy hidden around through urban sketching.
